Apply to be an expert

Use this service to apply to be an subject matter expert in specific subjects or industries to support Ofqual in regulating a range of qualifications.

Ofqual is looking for people from different backgrounds to apply to become a subject matter expert. Such as:

  • an assessor, examiner, or moderator
  • extensive skills and experience in a particular industry
  • a teacher, lecturer, trainer, practitioner, or an academic

Before you start

You can read the subject matter experts guidance for further information.

You will be asked to provide the following:

  • Personal details
  • Qualifications
  • Work history
  • Subjects that you can provide expertise on
  • Evidence of expertise
  • Conflicts of interest
  • Identity and verification checks

You will need qualification dates and scanned copies of certificates, employment dates and scanned copies of identification documents.

You can save your application and come back to it at any time.
