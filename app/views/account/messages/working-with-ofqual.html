Congratulations on joining Ofqual's subject matter specialist community.

We have now added you to the list of subject matter specialists.

You will be able to work with us on projects related to your approved subjects when they become available.

How we appoint subject matter specialists

Projects become available as and when Ofqual requires.

Ofqual will use the information provided in your application to match you to upcoming projects and contact you to discuss the work.

You can then review the project and decide if you would like to work on it with us.

Joining the list of subject matter specialists is not a guarantee of any work during your tenure.

What does the work involve?

The work can include:

  • reviewing the content of qualifications and the quality of their assessments
  • providing specialist opinions to assess the validity of qualifications and assessments
  • working on large-scale projects where qualifications are being developed
  • contributing to research projects
  • contributing to panel meetings to discuss a qualification review
  • completing a written report

Pay and travel

Pay is usually a standard daily rate, which we will confirm before you agree to do any work.

The majority of projects do not require travel, as most are done remotely. If we need you to travel, we will discuss this and any expenses with you.

Applying for more subjects or occupational areas

You cannot apply for more subjects or occupational areas. This functionality will be available in the future.

You will be contacted when we make updates to the service.

Contacting Ofqual

If you need to contact us about anything related to projects or subject matter specialists, send us a message from your account.
