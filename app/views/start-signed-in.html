Apply to provide expertise prototype

The service is for experienced teachers/trainers/lecturers, assessors and industry professionals.

Use this service to apply to be an advisor in specific subjects to support Ofqual, in regulating qualifications, apprenticeships and national assessments.

You can save your application and come back to it at any time.

Before you start

You will be asked to provide the following information:

  • Contact information
  • Education and qualifications
  • Work history
  • Types of expertise
  • Areas you can advise on
  • Personal statement
  • Verify your identity

You will need employment dates, certification details, employment information and verification documents to support your application.
