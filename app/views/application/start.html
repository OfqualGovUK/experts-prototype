Use this service to apply to be an subject matter expert in specific subjects or industries to support Ofqual in regulating a range of qualifications.

Ofqual is looking for people across different disciplines to apply to become a subject matter expert. Such as:

  • an assessor or an examiner
  • have extensive skills and experience in a particular industry
  • a teacher, lecturer, trainer, practitioner, or an academic

Before you start

You can read the subject matter experts guidance for further information.

You will be asked to provide the following:

  • Personal details
  • Education
  • Types of expertise
  • Work history
  • Areas you can advise on
  • Conflicts of interest
  • Identity and verification checks

You will need certification dates and scanned copies, employment dates and details and scanned copies of identification documents.

You can save your application and come back to it at any time.
