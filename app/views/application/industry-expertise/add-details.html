Industry or occupational expertise

Industry experts with the knowledge and skills of specific activities within an industry or occupation are required to support the review of qualifications and assessments.

Tell us about your industry expertise by describing your experience, skills, achievements, activities and responsibilities.

For example, a plumbing expert with in-depth knowledge of pipework diameters and tolerances would be able to inform Ofqual on whether a student or apprentice is assessed appropriately.
