Professional achievements

Industry experts with the knowledge and skills of specific activities within an industry or occupation are required to support the review of qualifications and assessments.

Tell us about your industry expertise by describing your experience, skills, achievements, activities and responsibilities.

For example, a plumbing expert with in-depth knowledge of pipework diameters and tolerances would be able to inform Ofqual on whether a student or apprentice is assessed appropriately.

{% set detailsHtml %}

This is your opportunity to provide information about your expertise. You can use this section to tell us about:

  • your experience, skills and current industry practices
  • specific industry practices to evidence your expertise
  • contribution to industry standards
  • experience leading or managing a team (mentoring or training)
  • understanding of your industry standards for your sector
  • understanding of your industry technologies and procedures
  • ability to provide clear and concise feedback
  • your skills and continuous learning and training
  • analytical and judgement skills that enable you to understand approaches and demonstrate your ability to provide conclusions
  • communication skills that demonstrate your ability to write clear evidence based reports

Evidence of your subject expertise can be covered in more detail in the areas of expertise section.
