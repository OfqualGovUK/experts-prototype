Areas where you can provide your expertise to Ofqual can be specific subjects. For example, Mathematics, Business Analysis and Engineering. If your expertise is not a specific subject, select your sector.

The subjects and sectors can cover different qualification types, such as:

  • GCSEs
  • AS/A levels
  • Apprenticeships
  • T levels
  • Other qualification
    • English for speakers of other languages (ESOL)
    • Essential digital skills
    • Functional skills
    • Vocationally related qualification
    • Performing arts graded examination

You will be able to add multiple subjects or sectors.
