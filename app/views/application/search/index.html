Subjects where you can provide your expertise to Ofqual can be specific subjects. For example, Mathematics, Business Analysis and Engineering. If your expertise is not a specific subject, select your sector.

Areas where you can provide your expertise to Ofqual can be categorised into a 'subject'.

This may include academic subjects, vocational fields of work, or specific qualifications such as end-point assessments and T-levels.

For example, Mathematics, Business Analysis, Asbestos, Accounting or English Language.

The subjects you select can cover different qualification types and levels, such as:

  • Mathematics - GCSE - Levels 1 and 2
  • Business Analysis - End-Point assessment - Level 4
  • Asbestos - Vocationally related qualification - Levels 1, 2 and 3

Your subject does not need to be linked to a qualification type or qualification level if you do not relate your expertise to a specific qualification.

If your subject expertise can be linked to a qualification type or a qualification level, you will be able to add multiple of these, as applicable to your expertise.
