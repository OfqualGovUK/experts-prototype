The expertise that you can provide to Ofqual may include, academic subjects, vocational fields of work, or specific qualifications such as end-point assessments and T-levels.

  • Mathematics - GCSE - Levels 1 and 2
  • Business Analysis - End-Point assessment - Level 4
  • Asbestos - Vocationally related qualification - Levels 1, 2 and 3
  • Drama - GCE A/AS level - Level 3

If your subject expertise can be linked to a qualification type or a qualification level, you will be able to add multiple of these.
